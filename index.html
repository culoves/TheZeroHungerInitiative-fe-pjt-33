About Us

The Zero Hunger Initiative strives to eliminating hunger in our communities, one day at a time, through donations from people like you!
We do this this with the aim of reducing hunger-induced crimes and killings as well as prevent malnourishment of indigent kids in the society due to lack of good food and proper nutrition.

Eliminate Hunger, One Donation At A Time!

Due to the ever rising inflation of the Nigerian economy, a high percentage of the population find it increasingly difficult to feed as well as they should. We at The Zero Hunger Initiative have found that sharing from what we have, no matter how little, helps to make our world a better place.

Can’t Donate Food?
Don’t Worry, You Can Still Be of Help.
